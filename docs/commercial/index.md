# For commercial use

Awen Weave is dual-licensed. Most organisations using it for community
or open work choose the AGPLv3 path; organisations needing proprietary
terms choose the commercial license.

## When you need the commercial license

The AGPLv3 license is sufficient for most uses. You need a commercial
license if any of the following are true:

- You want to use Awen Weave in a proprietary system where keeping your
  modifications and derivative work private is a requirement.
- You are subject to procurement, regulatory, or contractual constraints
  that exclude AGPL-licensed software (common in Tier 1 construction,
  regulated infrastructure, and some enterprise IT environments).
- You want indemnity, formal support agreements, service-level commitments,
  or warranties that the AGPLv3 does not provide.
- You want to embed Awen Weave in a commercial product where AGPL's
  network-use clause would require publishing the surrounding system.

If none of these apply, the AGPLv3 path is open to you under the
[For Developers](../developers/index.md) terms — no agreement with
Awen Weave Limited needed.

## What the commercial license grants

A commercial license is a private agreement between your organisation
and Awen Weave Limited. Typical terms include:

- **Proprietary use rights** — use and modify Awen Weave without the
  AGPL copyleft obligations applying to your modifications or
  derivative work.
- **Support and maintenance** — commitments around version upgrades,
  security patches, and response times for issues.
- **Indemnity** — warranty that the framework code is owned and
  licensable by Awen Weave Limited, with indemnity against third-party
  IP claims on the framework itself.
- **Service-level commitments** — uptime and incident-response terms
  where Awen Weave Limited is operating components on your behalf
  (where applicable).

Exact terms are negotiated per agreement, shaped by your use case,
deployment context, and operational requirements.

## What the commercial license does not grant

For clarity:

- **It does not grant rights over content in your instance.** Whatever
  data you put into your Awen Weave instance remains yours; the
  commercial license covers the framework, not your content.
- **It does not grant exclusivity.** Awen Weave Limited retains the
  right to license the framework to other organisations under the same
  or different terms.
- **It does not grant trademark rights.** The "AWEN WEAVE" name and any
  associated marks remain trademarks of Awen Weave Limited; commercial
  licensees use the framework but do not use the framework's brand to
  identify their own derivative products.

## Who Awen Weave Limited is

Awen Weave Limited is a UK private limited company, in formation as of
May 2026. Sole director: Huw Thomas. The company exists specifically
to hold the framework IP, grant commercial licenses, and provide
support to commercial licensees.

The company sits within the wider portfolio of Huw Thomas's ventures,
which includes With A W Limited (consulting) and other operational
entities held via With Aw Holdings Limited. The separation between
Awen Weave Limited (the framework IP holder) and the operating
community of each Awen Weave instance is structural — the company has
no claim on the content of any instance.

## How to start a commercial conversation

Get in touch:

- **Email:** huw@awenweave.com
- **Subject:** *Awen Weave commercial license enquiry*

Useful information to include in a first message:

- Your organisation and your role
- The nature of the intended deployment (community-facing, internal,
  product-embedded, etc.)
- The scale (one instance, multiple, internal-only, public-facing, etc.)
- The reason AGPLv3 is not the right fit for your context
- Any timing constraints

Initial conversation typically takes one 30-45 minute call to
understand the use case; subsequent steps depend on what's needed.

## Pricing

Commercial license pricing is set per agreement, based on deployment
context (scale, internal-or-external, support requirements). There is
no published price list at this stage. Initial enquiries are free; a
proposal is provided after the first conversation has established the
shape of the deployment.

## Pattern, not product

A reminder of the boundary: Awen Weave is licensed as a *pattern* —
the framework code, the conceptual articulation, the constitutional
principles. Awen Weave Limited does not operate instances on behalf of
licensees. Each commercial licensee operates their own instance(s),
configures them for their domain, and curates their own content. The
commercial license is for the framework that enables the work, not for
the work itself.

If you're looking for a managed-service deployment of Awen Weave —
where Awen Weave Limited operates the instance for you — that is a
separate conversation. The default commercial license is self-operated.
