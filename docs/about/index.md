# What Awen Weave is

Awen Weave is the pattern. It describes how to weave together knowledge,
relationships, place, data and human insight into a coherent living system
without silencing the contradictions, without privileging the algorithmic
over the human, and without losing the thread of where each piece came
from.

It is a framework for the kind of work that benefits from being done
patiently, with discipline, by people who care about getting it right —
rather than the kind of work that benefits from being fast, automated,
and indifferent to provenance.

## The shape

Awen Weave names its parts in Welsh because the conceptual lineage is
Welsh — the visible authority and explained judgements of the
[Hywel Dda Cyfraith](welsh-rooted.md) (Law of Hywel) tradition, and the
place-based contradiction-tolerant trust of Welsh mythological thinking
about land and continuity. The Welsh names are structural, not
decorative; they signal that this is a particular tradition of thought
about how knowledge should be held and contracts should be honoured.

The five-part architecture, with one-line introductions:

- **Llys** — the interaction and decision layer. The court where judgement
  happens visibly.
- **Craidd** — the place-based trust core. Curated, provenance-bound
  knowledge with explicit boundaries.
- **IDRIS** — the reasoning and orchestration layer. Synthesis across
  Craidd and Prawf, named for [Cadair Idris](welsh-rooted.md).
- **Prawf** — the obligation and proof layer. Append-only, hash-chained,
  visible.
- **Craffter** — the pattern-level observation layer. Sees patterns;
  stays advisory; never controls.

## The principles

Three statements that hold the pattern together. They are not
implementation choices; they are constitutional. Anything that violates
them is not Awen Weave.

1. **Contracts are the source of obligation.** What you are required to
   do comes from agreements that are visible, traceable, and explainable
   — not from machine inference about what you ought to do.
2. **Humans are the source of judgement.** Where a decision needs to be
   made, a person makes it. Automated tools surface patterns and surface
   options; they do not decide.
3. **Learning remains advisory.** Observations from pattern analysis
   inform humans; they do not feed back into canonical state without
   human review. Craffter sees; it does not write.

## Pattern, not product

Awen Weave is the pattern. Individual deployments — instances — apply the
pattern to specific domains and specific places. The
[Dolgellau Town Dataset](../instances/dolgellau-town-dataset.md) is the
first; others will follow in other places and other domains.

The boundary matters: the pattern is licensed by Huw-Lab (under AGPL +
commercial dual); each instance's *content* is owned by its operating
community. The community owns what is woven; Huw-Lab maintains the loom.
